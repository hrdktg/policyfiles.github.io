Privacy Policy

Introduction
This Privacy Policy describes how Bug Zapper ("we," "our," or "the App") handles user information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a transparent, safe gaming experience.
Information Collection and Use
We do not collect, store, or process any personal information or user data.
Our App:

Does not require account creation or login
Does not collect names, email addresses, or contact information
Does not track user behavior or gameplay statistics
Does not collect device identifiers or location data
Does not use analytics or tracking tools
Does not share any information with third parties

Permissions
Our App only requests permissions that are essential for core gameplay functionality. We do not access sensitive information or use permissions beyond their intended purpose. Any requested permissions are used solely to enable the game features and are never used to collect or transmit user data.
Third-Party Services
We do not integrate any third-party services, including:

Advertising networks
Analytics platforms
Social media integrations
Cloud services or external databases

Data Storage
Since we do not collect any user data, there is no data stored on our servers. Any game progress or settings are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted or backed up externally.
Children's Privacy
Our App does not collect any information from users of any age, including children under 13. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.
Security
While we do not collect user data, we are committed to ensuring the App is free from malicious code, does not abuse device resources, and does not misuse network capabilities.
Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
